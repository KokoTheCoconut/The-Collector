Will I be anonymous?

The articles produced will be anonymous because by sending an article, you agree to lose ownership of your story. You will however be informed if your text is used and will see the final version before it is published. We will accept comments and feedback.

What kind of text is accepted?

In both our articles and in the Collector's Public Domain, you can put in any text as long as it follows the guidelines. Any text that does otherwise will be removed without warning. Here are our guidlines:

    Guidlines:

  1. The text cannot contain cuss words or ill intended meanings behind slangs
  2. The text should not attack anyone of any sexuality, ethnicity, gender or age
  3. The text cannot be political
